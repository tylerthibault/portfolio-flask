About Me

My name is Tyler Thibault, and I have a wife and two kids. I served in the Navy for 5 years and after serving in the Navy, I moved to Indonesia, where I had actually lived as a child. During my time there, I became a school teacher, which ignited a passion for education that has stayed with me ever since. When I moved back to the US, I transitioned into software development and quickly found that my passion for teaching could be applied to this field as well. I now work as a teacher of programmers, helping the next generation of developers to grow and thrive in their careers.
